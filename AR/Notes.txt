What you now have access to for HUD skins

For any target or mouseover, AR now knows:

Identity:
name
level
faction
creature type
classification (elite/rare/boss)
pet / player / NPC / civilian / battle pet

Health:
current HP
max HP
%HP

Power:
current
max
type (mana/energy/focus/rage/etc.)
%power

Threat:
UnitThreatSituation("player", unitID) (0–3)

Flags:
PvP
PvP FFA
tap-denied (grey mob)
in-range
distance in yards

Casting:
spell name
interruptible flag
cast/channel end time

Auras:
buffCount (0–40)
debuffCount (0–40)

Tooltip:
header
subheader
lines[]
“isQuestGiver”, “isVendor”, “isTrainer”
